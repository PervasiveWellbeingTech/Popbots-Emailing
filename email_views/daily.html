Chatbots Study Consent and Pre-study Survey
Pervasive Wellbeing Technology Lab, Stanford University

What the study is about: The purpose of this study is to understand how users interact with the chatbots that we have developed, as well as to obtain feedback about the experience and efficacy of chatting with the bots. 

What we will ask you to do: If you agree to be in this study, you will be asked to use different chatbots on a daily basis, and we expect it to take a total of 1-2 hours of your time. Our bots are simple, but with your help, we can improve them. Please try to interact with them as casually and naturally as possible. You will be compensated for successfully completing both this pre-study and a post-study questionnaire and you will receive additional compensation for each day you interact with the chatbots and complete a daily survey. Compensation will be provided via an Amazon gift card at the end of the study.

Confidentiality: Your responses to the survey questions and your messages with our chatbots will be kept private. Any information that we make public will not include information that will make it possible to identify you.

Please note that our bots are designed to help relieve daily stress and are not designed to treat clinical mental illness. If are experiencing any serious mental health issues, please contact a mental health professional.
 
By checking YES, you are consenting to participating in this study.
